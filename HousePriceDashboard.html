Key Findings
📐
Area is King
House area accounts for 46.6% of predictive importance in the Random Forest model — by far the single biggest driver of price. Bigger homes command significantly higher prices.
❄️
AC Adds Real Value
Air conditioning ranks 3rd in importance at ~6%. Homes with AC command an average price premium of ₹8–12 lakhs compared to similar homes without it.
🛁
Bathrooms Beat Bedrooms
Bathrooms (15.4% importance) outweigh bedrooms (4.6%). More bathrooms signal luxury and space efficiency — buyers value them more than raw bedroom count.
🔥
Hot Water — Irrelevant?
Surprisingly, hot water heating had near-zero impact on price despite seeming like a premium feature. Perhaps it's considered standard in this market.
📊
Linear Beats Random Forest
The simpler Linear Regression (R²=0.653) outperformed Random Forest (R²=0.614) on this dataset. With only 545 samples, simpler models generalize better.
💼
Business Recommendation
For sellers: invest in adding AC and a bathroom before listing — these give the highest ROI. Buyers should focus on sq ft per rupee as the primary value metric.
